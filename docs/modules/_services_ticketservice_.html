DesiredQueuePosition

DesiredQueuePosition: "FIRST" | "MIDDLE" | "LAST"
  • Defined in services/TicketService.ts:191

Represents a position in the queue where the ticket should go when returning it to the queue. They should either be the first in line, somewhere in the center (when they will be back soon), or the last in line.
